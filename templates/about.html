About Foodie

Foodie is a recipe scaler and meal planner for people who want to plan meals and adjust portions without manual math.

MVP features

  • Recipe search and selection (Edamam Recipe Search API)
  • Servings-based scaling for ingredient quantities
  • Weekly meal plan: one recipe per calendar day
  • Calories and macros (protein, carbs, fat) per recipe

Data source

Primary nutrition and recipe metadata come from the Edamam Recipe Search API (see Edamam developer documentation for endpoints and free-tier limits). A USDA FoodData Central backup is optional for future work.
